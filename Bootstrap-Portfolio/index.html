About Me

Hello, my name is Eric Ward and I want to tell you a few things about me. I was born and raised in Delaware, and the youngest of 7 kids. While in 8th grade, I went on a field trip to Russia for 10 days. That was in 1991.

In high school I took 4 years of Spanish class and I also had friends from diverse cultures and backgrounds. When I was 19, I was a missionary for my church (The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints) in Paraguay where I spoke Spanish and learned the Guarani language.

A few years later, in 2001, I married a beautiful and intelligent girl from Peru. We met in Utah. We have 5 kids; 3 boys and 2 girls, and my family is the treasure in my life. As a family, we love to travel. We have been to Peru a few times, Mexico a couple of times, gone on two cruises, Disneyland, Dana Point (CA), Oregon, and Delaware.

Most recently, we went to Portland, Oregon to visit my brother-in-law at Christmastime. One day we traveled to Seaside, OR and enjoyed the beach and got to eat some fish and chips.
